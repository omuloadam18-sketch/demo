Who We Are

CBC education that grows with every child

We are a young and steadily growing institution, and we wear that with pride — every term brings new classrooms, new resources, and new milestones for our learners.

  • Full CBC delivery from Pre-Primary through Grade 6
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Qualified, TSC-registered teaching staff
  • Strong focus on literacy, numeracy and life skills
  • A safe, nurturing environment in Embakasi, Nairobi
